Nutrition Plan

A healthy eating plan gives your body the nutrients it needs every day while staying within your daily calorie goal for weight loss. A healthy eating plan also will lower your risk for heart disease and other health conditions. It emphasizes vegetables, fruits, whole grains, and fat-free or low-fat dairy products.

Performance & Fitness

Experts define physical fitness as "one's ability to execute daily activities with optimal performance, endurance, and strength with the management of disease, fatigue, and stress and reduced sedentary behavior." This description goes beyond being able to run quickly or lift heavy weights.

Routine Programme
